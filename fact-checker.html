The internet is not the truth, yet many believe in the words, images and ideas presented on the internet. Facts rarely matter, because facts have the peculiarity of being very boring. On the other hand, information and data are distorted to such an extent that in the end a non-factual perception emerges that feels like the truth, but is a mirage of what in reality can be reconstructed as facts. We are developing an algorithm that shows to what extent a piece of information corresponds mathematically precisely to a fact. To do this, we use only the data stock of the internet, which represents the ideas of the many. ----- Das Internet ist nicht die Wahrheit, dennoch glauben vielen an die Worte, Bilder und Ideen, die im Internet präsentiert werden.
